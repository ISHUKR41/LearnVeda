# Force and Laws of Motion — Chapter Content Module

## Purpose
This directory contains the complete educational content for the **Class 9 Science Chapter: Force and Laws of Motion**.
It includes structured topic data, a comprehensive question bank, and TypeScript types
for rendering the chapter study page.

## Structure
- `content.ts` — All 5 topics with subtopics, explanations, key formulas, and real-world examples.
- `questions.ts` — Full question bank: MCQs, Short Answer, Long Answer, and Thinking Questions per topic.
- `types.ts` — TypeScript interfaces used by both files.

## Source Material
Content synthesized from three published Google Docs research reports:
1. "Force and Motion Deep Dive" — Comprehensive topic explanations
2. "Force and Laws of Motion Research Request" — Additional analysis and questions
3. "Comprehensive Educational Report" — Hindi + English bilingual supplement

## NCERT Alignment
All content follows the **NCERT Class 9 Science Chapter 9** (2025–26 syllabus).
Topics are ordered to match the NCERT textbook progression.
